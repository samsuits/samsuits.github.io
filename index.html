FIG. 04 — SELECTED STRUCTURAL WORK

Three jobs where the foundation moved while the building stayed open

01
INOVALON — 2023 TO PRESENT

Migrating the load-bearing version of PHP, mid-tenancy

The Claims Management Platform processes high-volume healthcare claims data under HIPAA, around the clock, for live customers. Moving its core PHP runtime from version 7 to 8 meant touching typed properties, modern syntax, and dependency compatibility across a codebase nobody gets to take offline.

As backend engineer, I owned the migration path end-to-end — sequencing the changes, coordinating with the React frontend and QA teams, and validating each step against production traffic patterns before it shipped.

Result

Full runtime migration completed with zero service disruption to a live healthcare SaaS platform under active compliance audit.

02
EZYVET — 2021 TO 2023

A second 7→8 migration, this time against a 100% uptime promise

ezyVet's veterinary practice-management platform runs as a 24/7 cloud SaaS product with a contractual 100% uptime commitment to its customers. A second PHP major-version migration in my career, this one with even less room for error — any service interruption is visible to every clinic using the platform in real time.

I also designed and documented the API integrations that let the platform extend through third-party systems, and acted as the technical liaison between product management and the development squad, translating business priorities into engineering sequencing.

Result

Migration delivered without breaching the platform's 100% uptime SLA.
